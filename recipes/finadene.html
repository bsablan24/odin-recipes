Finadene

Description

A popular sauce used in Guam.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the soy sauce, lemon juice, green onions, and red pepper flakes. Let stand for about 15 minutes to allow flavors to marry.
  2. Enjoy with meat and rice!
