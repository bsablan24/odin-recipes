Easy Tasty Nachos

Description

A quick delicious meal that is easy to assemble. One of my favorite comfort foods!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of desired tortilla chips
  • cooked chicken breast or thighs
  • copious amount of mexican cheese blend (1 large bag)
  • 1 small can sliced olives
  • 1-2 stems of green onion
  • 1 tbsp lightly chopped cinlantro
  • tomato (diced)
  • sliced jalapeno (fresh or pickled)
  • sour cream
  • guacamole
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil (for easy cleanup).
  2. Spread tortilla chips on baking sheet as evenly as possible.
  3. Sprinkle olives, jalapeno, and green onions over the chips.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the chips. The more the better!
  5. Sprinkle more olives, jalapeno, and green onions over cheese.
  6. Set oven to broil (500 degrees). Place on middle rack and watch. Should only take a few minutes. Remove from oven when cheese is melted and chips are lightly toasted on edges of pan.
  7. Sprinkle diced tomato and chopped cilantro on top of nachos.
  8. Add guacamole and sour cream on top or have on the side.
  9. Don't forget the salsa and enjoy!
